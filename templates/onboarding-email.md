# Onboarding Email Templates

Use these templates as starting points for customer onboarding sequences.

---

## Email 1: Welcome (Immediate)

**Goal:** Excitement + first quick win

```
Subject: You're in! Here's your first step

Hi [Name],

Welcome! I'm so excited you're here.

I know you're eager to get started, so let me make this simple:

**Your ONE task right now:** [Single, quick action]

That's it. Don't worry about anything else yet.

Once you've done that, reply to this email and let me know - I love
hearing from new [members/clients/students]!

Talk soon,
[Your name]

P.S. Keep an eye on your inbox - I'll send you the next step tomorrow.
```

---

## Email 2: Quick Win (Day 1-2)

**Goal:** Build momentum with easy success

```
Subject: Did you do the thing? (+ your next step)

Hi [Name],

How did [first task] go?

If you haven't done it yet, no worries - here's a quick recap: [brief
instructions]

If you're ready for more, here's your next step:

**Today's task:** [Second action - still relatively easy]

This should take about [time estimate]. By the end, you'll [benefit/outcome].

You're building momentum - keep it up!

[Your name]
```

---

## Email 3: Deeper Dive (Day 3-5)

**Goal:** Get into the main content/process

```
Subject: Now let's get into the good stuff

Hi [Name],

By now you've [recap of what they've accomplished].

Nice work! You're ready for the next level.

**This week's focus:** [Main task or content area]

Here's how to approach it:

1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

**Common question at this stage:** "[Anticipated question]"
[Answer to that question]

If you get stuck, [how to get help].

You've got this,
[Your name]
```

---

## Email 4: Check-In (Day 7)

**Goal:** Catch strugglers, celebrate winners

```
Subject: Quick check-in: How's it going?

Hi [Name],

It's been a week since you started - wanted to check in!

**If things are going great:** Amazing! Keep up the momentum. Your next
milestone is [what's coming up].

**If you're stuck:** Totally normal. The most common place people get
tripped up is [common issue]. Here's how to get unstuck: [solution or
resource]

**If you haven't started yet:** No judgment! Life happens. When you're
ready, just [simple re-entry point].

Hit reply and let me know where you're at - I read every response.

Cheering you on,
[Your name]
```

## Usage Notes

- **Personalize the bracketed sections** for your specific offer
- **Adjust timing** based on your program structure
- **A/B test subject lines** - these are starting points
- **Track which emails get replies** - that's engagement signal
